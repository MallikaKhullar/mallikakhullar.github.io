About Me

I lead product for multimodal Machine Translation at Apple, building Apple Intelligence Live Translation — the system that powers real-time translation across iMessage, FaceTime, Phone calls, AirPods, Camera, Safari, and more. My work ships transformer-based encoder-decoder models to billions of devices, optimized for on-device inference through techniques like attention pruning, quantization, and adapter-based specialization — sitting at the intersection of foundation models, privacy-preserving design, and platform-scale product thinking.

Before Apple, I built products in healthcare — from India's largest telehealth platform (MediBuddy, 30M+ users) to a parenting and pregnancy health community (Tinystep, 1M+ users). I also co-founded a tech-for-good nonprofit and built infrastructure at Amazon that became Prime Video. I hold an MS from Stanford (Management Science & Engineering, 4.0 GPA) and a BTech in CS from Manipal Institute of Technology.

I'm passionate about eliminating resource, information, and language gaps at scale.

Focus Areas

System-Wide Translation

Translation natively integrated across the OS — in Camera, Photos, Safari, Messages, and system-wide text selection — making it feel like a platform capability, not an app.

Multi-Platform Reach

Shipping translation across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and AirPods — adapting the experience to each device's unique interaction model and constraints.

Multimodal AI

Translation spanning text, voice, vision, camera, and real-time conversation — from end-to-end speech-to-text translation with prosody awareness to visual OCR pipelines, each modality requiring distinct model architectures and latency constraints.

Privacy-Preserving AI

On-device foundation models optimized via low-bit palletization, attention pruning, and adapter specialization — delivering SOTA translation quality without user data for training, backed by Private Cloud Compute for workloads that exceed on-device capacity.

Developer Platform & APIs

Opening translation as a platform capability for third-party developers — the Translation API and Call Translation API let any app integrate Apple's on-device ML models.

ML Research to Product

Translating cutting-edge research into shipped features — from gender-aware disambiguation and hallucination mitigation to efficient transformer inference via asymmetric encoder-decoder architectures and cross-attention optimization.
